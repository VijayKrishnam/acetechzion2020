About ACEM

Aditya College of Engineering, Madanapalle under the umbrella of Veda Educational Society was Established in the year 2009 to impart excellent technical and value based Education under the able and dynamic leadership of Sri R.Ramamohan Reddy, Secretary & Correspondent and Sri .M. Nagamalla Reddy, President. This High Performing and Fast growing College is led by the Principal Dr. S. Ramalinga Reddy who is a visionary and is responsible for establishing the culture in the college, setting the academic tone and creating an atmosphere that nurtures excellent teachers as well as students, with the support of the management.

This institution has been relentlessly working on grooming young minds and preparing them for delivering value to the society at large with the motto “Explore, Experience, Excel”. The Institution offers the courses of ECE, CSE with an intake of 120 students and EEE, ME,CE with an intake of 60 students.
